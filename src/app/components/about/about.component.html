Sam is currently as Associate Software Engineer at Charles Schwab, Inc. He works on the Statusticians team supporting the Schwab Advisor Center (SAC) with Schwab Technology Services (STS). Outside of the digital world, Sam's interests include playing sports, cars, hiking, enjoying the outdoors, weightlifting, studying history, and learning about nutrition.

Growing up between California, the DC metro area, Tennessee, and most recently Florida, working in Austin, TX at Charles Schwab gives another opportunity for him to experience this great nation and all it has to offer.
